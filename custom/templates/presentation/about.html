Startup
Ecosystem

Though the very heart of ecosystem are StartUps and Investors, surrounding players are playing an important role in providing friendly and high-quality environment, supporting ease and security on doing businesses and deals and improving resources in both senses of quality and quantity.

Startup

Thai companies whose solutions, services and platforms provide a business advantage in the highly competitive market and a better life for people.

Investor

Persons or business bodies committing capital with the expectation of returns from the growth of competitive Thai companies.

Supporting Organization

Surrounding players providing friendly and high-quality environment, supporting ease and security on doing businesses and deals and improving resources in both senses of quality and quantity.

Emerging technologies especially in ICT is causing paradigm shift in economic and much more competitiveness in every vertical. Companies who are not transforming and adapting to the new battlefield quickly enough, will be faded and replaced with the emerging companies. Thailand needs a new breed of warriors at business frontiers who can move faster with the new tools and lead all Thai industries to the new age of digital transformation.

What we do

Initiated by SET, NEW is a Thailand online community platform which consists of innovative solutions, services, and platforms created by Thai companies, Thailand-based Investors and supporting players in Thai Startup Ecosystem. We are aiming at bringing together all key players in Thailand StartUp Ecosystem to create more visibility and to provide an effective communication platform reaching key players in Thailand.
